Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

01 AI chat data

Messages you send to XP's "Whisper" chat feature are transmitted to Google's Generative Language API (Gemini) for processing. Google acts as a sub-processor under its API Terms and does not use this content to train its models.

02 Storage & sync

Your chat messages, AI responses, tasks, energy logs, and usage counters are also stored in Google Firebase Firestore, scoped to your unique account ID, to let you resume conversations across devices.

Account authentication uses Firebase Auth (email, UID). Payments for premium features may be processed via RevenueCat and platform billing; we do not store full card numbers on our servers.

03 What we keep, for how long

  • Account (email, UID): kept while your account is active
  • Chat messages + AI responses: kept while your account is active
  • Anonymous security logs (auth events, rate-limit breaches): 30 days

04 Deletion

Tapping Wipe memory in Settings permanently deletes all your chat history, tasks, and progress from both your device and our servers within 24 hours. Tapping Delete account additionally deletes your authentication record.

You can also request deletion without the app at https://xpecosystem.com/xp/delete.

05 Crisis content

If your message contains signals of self-harm or suicidal ideation we do not send it to the AI. We instead show a link to international crisis resources (findahelpline.com). We keep an anonymous counter of these events for safety monitoring; we never store the text of the message.

06 Data we do NOT collect

  • Phone contacts, SMS, microphone, camera, precise location
  • Health sensor data, biometrics
  • Any data from users under 13

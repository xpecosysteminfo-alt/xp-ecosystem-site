How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your XP account using one of the following methods:

A In-App Deletion

You can delete your account directly in the XP app via:

Settings → Delete My Account

B Via Email

If you no longer have access to the app, send an email to:

✉️  contact@xpecosystem.com

Important: The request must be sent from the email address associated with your XP account. This is how we verify your identity.

📋

Data Deleted

Deleting your account will permanently remove the following data:

✕ Profile: Email address and account information
✕ Tasks: All your tasks and to-do items
✕ Progression: XP, levels, and all gamification data
🔒

Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data after account deletion, with the sole exception of financial transaction histories that we are required to keep by law.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the account deletion process, feel free to contact us:

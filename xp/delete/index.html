Last updated: April 2026

🗑️

How to delete your XP account

In the app

Settings → Delete Account → Confirm

This removes your account, chat history, tasks and progress within 24 hours, on-device and on our servers.

Can't access the app?

Email sam@xpecosystem.com from the address on your account with the subject "Delete my XP account".

We will confirm and complete deletion within 7 days.

📋

What gets deleted

  • Firebase Auth record (UID, email)
  • All chat sessions and threads (Firestore)
  • All tasks, energy logs, insights (Firestore)
  • Daily usage counters (Firestore)
  • Purchase history is retained for tax/legal reasons (anonymized)

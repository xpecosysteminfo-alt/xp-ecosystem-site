Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

01 Information We Collect

Iron Thumb is designed to work without account creation while respecting your privacy. Depending on how you use the app, we may collect:

  • No Account Data: The app does not require you to create an account to use it.
  • App Activity: Basic in-app events needed to operate, maintain, and improve the service.
  • Diagnostics: Device, crash, and performance data used to keep the app stable and secure.
  • Support Messages: If you contact us directly, we may receive the email address and message content you choose to send.

02 How We Use Information

We use collected information only for legitimate business purposes, including to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality without requiring user accounts.
  • Respond to support requests and user inquiries.
  • Monitor reliability, fix issues, and improve user experience.
  • Protect the service, users, and our systems from misuse.

03 Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

04 No Account Suppression Needed

Iron Thumb does not create user accounts as part of normal use, so there is no account suppression or account deletion workflow for this app. If you have a privacy question, you can still contact us using the details below.
